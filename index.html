About Bail My Tail

Since 1982, Bail My Tail has been a trusted name in Michigan's bail bond industry. Founded by Gene Butler, we've built our reputation on professionalism, integrity, and dedication to helping families during difficult times.

As Life Member #10 of the Professional Bail Agents of the United States and founders of the Michigan Professional Bail Agent's Association, we bring unmatched expertise to every case.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the bail bond process work?

We'll guide you through each step of the process, from paperwork to release, ensuring a smooth and efficient experience.

What information do I need to provide?

Basic information about the defendant and the case is required. Contact us for specific details about your situation.

How quickly can you help?

We work quickly to process your case and can often arrange release within hours of contact.

What are your payment options?

We offer flexible payment plans and accept various payment methods. Contact us to discuss your options.
